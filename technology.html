Old Technology

Cannon

The cannon is a 12th century large-caliber gun that has suffered several updates and is still used nowadays in different configurations such as ship cannons or tank cannons. It's classified as a type of artillery. It was widely used by many people. Cannons vary in gauge, effective range, mobility, rate of fire, angle of fire and firepower; different forms of cannon combine and balance these attributes in varying degrees.

Current Technology

B2 Spirit (Strategic Bomber)

Developed in the Cold War, the B2 Spirit is still the most advanced strategic bomber in the world. It can pass as almost invisible in radars running several miles over the world carring up to 40,000 pounds of weapons or bombs. It's the most expensive airplane in history.

General Atomics MQ-9 Reaper (drone)

General Atomics MQ-9 Reaper is a unmanned aerial vehicle (drone). It's the biggest of its category having 15x more cargo capacity than its predecessor.
